Which organisms survive when arriving to a new environment?

(A) The popular ones.

(B) The strongest one.

(C) ** The ones best adapted and fit for the ecosystem.

(D) The ones least adapted to the ecosystem.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Natural selection is a mechanism of evolution. Organisms that are more adapted to their environment are more likely to survive and pass on the genes that aided their success. This process causes species to change and diverge over time.

Concept note-2: -Living organisms are adapted to their environment. This means that the way they look, the way they behave, how they are built, or their way of life makes them suited to survive and reproduce in their habitats.

Concept note-3: -Structural adaptations are unique features of the body that help the animal survive in its environment. This includes body parts like feet, tails, ears, beaks, and wings.

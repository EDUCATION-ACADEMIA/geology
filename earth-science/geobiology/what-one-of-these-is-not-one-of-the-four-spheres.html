What one of these is NOT one of the four spheres?

(A) Geosphere

(B) Biosphere

(C) Atmosphere

(D) ** Hemisphere

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The names of the four spheres are derived from the Greek words for stone (litho), air or vapor (atmo), water (hydro), and life (bio). Ozonosphere is a part of atmosphere. That’s why it is not a sphere of the earth.

Concept note-2: -Thus Photosphere is not a layer of atmosphere.

Concept note-3: -Within the boundary of the Earth is a collection of four interdependent parts called “spheres“: the lithosphere, hydrosphere, biosphere, and atmosphere. The spheres are so closely connected that a change in one sphere often results in a change in one or more of the other spheres.

Concept note-4: -There are five main systems, or spheres, on Earth.

Concept note-5: -These four subsystems are called “spheres.” Specifically, they are the “lithosphere” (land), “hydrosphere” (water), “biosphere” (living things), and “atmosphere” (air). Each of these four spheres can be further divided into sub-spheres.

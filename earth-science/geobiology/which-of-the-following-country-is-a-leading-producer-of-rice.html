which of the following country is a leading producer of rice?

(A) ** China

(B) Pakistan

(C) Malaysia

(D) Egypt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The leading producer of rice is China. India ranks second in rice production. Indonesia, Bangladesh, Vietnam, Thailand, and Myanmar, are other major producers of rice.

Concept note-2: -China is the leading rice producer with 205.463 Million Metric Tons per year. India occupies next position in rice production after China. China is the world’s largest producer of rice. It is also the first country in the world to successfully produce hybrid rice, bred from two different types of plants.

Concept note-3: -Omri Wallach. Just 10 countries are responsible for almost 85% of the world’s rice production. China and India are the top two producers, accounting for more than half of the global total.

Concept note-4: -India is the second-largest producer of the crop after China. The world’s largest producer of rice accounts for more than thirty percent of world rice production.

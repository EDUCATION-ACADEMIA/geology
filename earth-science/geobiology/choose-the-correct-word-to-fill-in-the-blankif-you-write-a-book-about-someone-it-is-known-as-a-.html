Choose the correct word to fill in the blank.If you write a book about someone, it is known as a ____

(A) ** biography

(B) biology

(C) biologist

(D) biosphere

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is ‘Autobiography’. The most appropriate word one-word for the given group of words is ‘Autobiography’.

Concept note-2: -But there are some distinct differences. Simply put, a biography is the life history of an individual, written by someone else. An autobiography is the story of a person’s life, written by that person. And a memoir is a collection of memories written by the person themselves.

Concept note-3: -Writing a book about your life is called a memoir or an autobiography. The difference is that a memoir has lessons and an overall theme or message tied to the stories while an autobiography is a chronological account of your life.

Concept note-4: -Autobiography: an account of a person’s life written by that person. Biography: an account of someone’s life written by someone else.

Concept note-5: -Biographical writing is a specific form of writing and research that takes as its subject the lives of individuals. As such it concentrates on constructing personal histories, and places them within their social, political and historical context, in order to discover and explain the influences on an individual’s life.

GEOBIOLOGY

[SOURCES]
anything having to do with a region’s natural features, the population, its history, industries, etc.

(A) geometric

(B) ** geographic

(C) biothermic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A natural region (landscape unit) is a basic geographic unit. Usually, it is a region which is distinguished by its common natural features of geography, geology, and climate.

Concept note-2: -History is influenced by geographical conditions in many ways. Our diet, clothing, housing, occupation, in fact, all human life in any region depends to a large extent on its geographical characteristics.

Concept note-3: -The five themes of geography are location, place, region, movement, and human-environment interaction. The five themes enable you to discuss and explain people, places, and environments of the past and present.

Concept note-4: -These features include vegetation, climate, the local water cycle, and land formations. Geography doesn’t just determine whether humans can live in a certain area or not, it also determines people’s lifestyles, as they adapt to the available food and climate patterns.

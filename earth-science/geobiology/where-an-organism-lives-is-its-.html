Where an organism lives is its-

(A) niche

(B) ecosystem

(C) ** habitat

(D) home

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A habitat is a place where an organism makes its home. A habitat meets all the environmental conditions an organism needs to survive. For an animal, that means everything it needs to find and gather food, select a mate, and successfully reproduce.

Concept note-2: -A place where an organism or a community of organisms live, including the living and nonliving conditions of their surrounding environment, is called a habitat. The habitats of all living organisms make up the Earthâ€™s biosphere.

Concept note-3: -There are basically three types of habitat. They are aquatic habitat, terrestrial habitat and arboreal habitat.

Concept note-4: -Habitat is the natural home or environment of an animal, plant or any other organism.

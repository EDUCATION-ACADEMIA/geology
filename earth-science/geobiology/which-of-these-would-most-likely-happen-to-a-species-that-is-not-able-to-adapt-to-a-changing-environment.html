Which of these would most likely happen to a species that is not able to adapt to a changing environment?

(A) ** The species would become extinct over time

(B) The species would develop mutated cells

(C) The species would develop resistance to disease

(D) The species would change the environment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If conditions change more quickly than a species can evolve, however, and if members of that species lack the traits they need to survive in the new environment, the likely result will be extinction.

Concept note-2: -Which of these would most likely happen to a species that is not able to adapt to a changing environment? The species would become extinct over time.

Concept note-3: -Populations that fail to adapt fast enough eventually decline to extinction.

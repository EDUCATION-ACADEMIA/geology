Individual living thing

(A) ** Organism

(B) Population

(C) Community

(D) Ecosystem

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Organism: Individual living thing that may be made up of one or more organ systems.

Concept note-2: -An individual is one organism and is also one type of organism (e.g., human, cat, moose, palm tree, gray whale, tapeworm, or cow in our example).

Concept note-3: -Living things are divided into five kingdoms: animal, plant, fungi, protist and monera.

Concept note-4: -Organisms are individual living entities. For example, each tree in a forest is an organism. Single-celled prokaryotes and single-celled eukaryotes are also considered organisms and are typically referred to as microorganisms.

Concept note-5: -Human beings. Plants. Insects. Mammals. Mosses. Animals. Reptiles. Bacteria. More items

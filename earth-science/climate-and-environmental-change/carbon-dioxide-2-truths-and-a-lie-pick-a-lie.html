Carbon Dioxide-2 Truths and a Lie, Pick a lie

(A) The concentration of carbon dioxide in Earth’s atmosphere is at a record-breaking level

(B) For the very first time in history, carbon dioxide levels have exceeded 400 parts per million

(C) ** Carbon dioxide is a pollutant in the atmosphere

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -C. Carbon dioxide is a pollutant in the atmosphere. Answer C is a LIE. The concentration of CO2 in Earth’s atmosphere is at a record-breaking level.

Concept note-2: -Is carbon dioxide an air pollutant? Carbon dioxide is not considered an air pollutant, as it occurs naturally in the air. However, the CO2 concentration in the atmosphere has significantly increased due to human activity. Carbon dioxide is a greenhouse gas; alongside nitrous oxide (N2O), and methane (CH4).

Concept note-3: -Because, its excessive amount in atmosphere lead to global warming. The heat entering the earth’s atmosphere from sun is trapped by carbon dioxide hence leading to rise in temperature. Hence its excessive amount is considered as harmful to environment. So, it is considered as a pollutant.

Concept note-4: -Carbon dioxide is considered as a pollutant. Although, carbon dioxide is a natural component of air, its concentration in the air has dramatically increased. This is because of the large scale burning of fossil fuels, which increase the amount of carbon dioxide in the air.

Concept note-5: -Carbon monoxide (CO) is a clear, odourless gas. Smoke and exhaust fumes often contain carbon monoxide. Carbon monoxide is a common air pollutant.

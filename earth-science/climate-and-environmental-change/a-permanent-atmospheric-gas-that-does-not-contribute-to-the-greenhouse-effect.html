A permanent atmospheric gas that does not contribute to the greenhouse effect.

(A) ** Oxygen

(B) Chloroflurocarbons (CFCs)

(C) Nitrous Oxide

(D) Methane

Concept note-2: -Most of the gas in the atmosphere is nitrogen and oxygen, which cannot absorb heat and contribute to the greenhouse effect.

Concept note-3: -Carbon dioxide, methane and water vapour are greenhouse gases. But the main constituents of the atmosphere nitrogen and oxygen are not greenhouse gases.

Which of these is an observable impact of global climate change?

(A) Glaciers are shrinking

(B) Growing seasons are becoming longer in some areas

(C) The range of insects is increasing

(D) ** all of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -rising maximum temperatures. rising minimum temperatures. rising sea levels. higher ocean temperatures.

Concept note-3: -Climate impacts are already harming health, through air pollution, disease, extreme weather events, forced displacement, pressures on mental health, and increased hunger and poor nutrition in places where people cannot grow or find sufficient food.

Concept note-4: -Symptoms of climate change are all around us: extreme weather, diminishing sea ice, year after year of record-breaking warmth, drought, fires, and stress to ecosystems. Many of these consequences will create hardship for humans.

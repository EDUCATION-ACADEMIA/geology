Which of the following is NOT evidence of global warming?

(A) Sea level rising

(B) Loss of Arctic Sea Ice

(C) ** Less Water Vapor in the Air

(D) More extreme weather events

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Increased agricultural productivity worldwide.

Concept note-2: -Plants are blooming earlier in the spring. Mountain glaciers are melting worldwide, and snow cover is declining in the Northern Hemisphere (Learn more here and here). Greenland’s ice sheet-which holds about 8 percent of Earth’s fresh water-is melting at an accelerating rate (learn more).

Concept note-3: -Some people mistakenly believe water vapor is the main driver of Earth’s current warming. But increased water vapor doesn’t cause global warming. Instead, it’s a consequence of it. Increased water vapor in the atmosphere amplifies the warming caused by other greenhouse gases.

Concept note-4: -Out of the following oxygen is not a greenhouse gas because it does not trap radiations from the sun to raise the average temperature of the earth but carbon dioxide, nitrous oxide, methane etc. have created a thermal blanket around the earth trapping more heat and in turn raising the average temperature of the earth.

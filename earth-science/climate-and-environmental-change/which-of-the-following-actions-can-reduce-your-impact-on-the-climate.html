Which of the following actions can reduce your impact on the climate?

(A) ** Ride a bike to school or work

(B) Leaving the lights on all the time

(C) Going on an airplane every year

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Choosing a bike over a car just once a day reduces the average person’s carbon emissions from transportation by 67%. More than half of all daily trips are also less than three miles long-a perfect distance for a climate-friendly bike ride.

Concept note-2: -Walk, bike, carpool or take mass transit more often. You’ll save one pound of carbon dioxide for every mile you don’t drive! You can save 2, 400 pounds of carbon dioxide per year by recycling just half of your household waste.

Concept note-3: -Put a price on carbon. End fossil fuel subsidies. Build low-carbon, resilient cities. Increase energy efficiency and use of renewable energy. Implement climate-smart agriculture and nurture forest landscapes. 18-Mar-2015

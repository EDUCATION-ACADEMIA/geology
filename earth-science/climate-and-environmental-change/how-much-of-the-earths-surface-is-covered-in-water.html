How much of the Earth’s surface is covered in water?

(A) 50%

(B) ** 70%

(C) 85%

(D) 92%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -About 71 percent of the Earth’s surface is water-covered, and the oceans hold about 96.5 percent of all Earth’s water.

Concept note-2: -The ocean covers more than 70 percent of the surface of our planet. It’s hard to imagine, but about 97 percent of the Earth’s water can be found in our ocean.

Concept note-3: -The ocean is a huge body of saltwater that covers about 71 percent of Earth’s surface. The planet has one global ocean, though oceanographers and the countries of the world have traditionally divided it into four distinct regions: the Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, and Arctic oceans.

Concept note-4: -The Earth’s surface is covered in more water than by land. About 70% of the surface is covered in water.

Concept note-5: -Over 68 percent of the fresh water on Earth is found in icecaps and glaciers, and just over 30 percent is found in ground water. Only about 0.3 percent of our fresh water is found in the surface water of lakes, rivers, and swamps.

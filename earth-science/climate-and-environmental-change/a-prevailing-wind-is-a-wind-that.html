A prevailing wind is a wind that

(A) blows from north to south

(B) ** blows in and area regularly

(C) is at the equator at all times

(D) carries boats along the ocean currents

Concept note-1: -Prevailing winds are winds that blow from a single direction over a specific area of the Earth. Areas where prevailing winds meet are called convergence zones.

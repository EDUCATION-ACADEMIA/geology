Things you can do to help decrease global warming include:

(A) Eat more ice cream.

(B) Keep your freezer door open.

(C) Keep your air conditioner on high.

(D) ** Turn off your lights when you’re not using them.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Turn off the lights. Close doors immediately so heat does not escape. Take short showers. Walk or bike if you can (instead of having your parents drive you). Turn off your computer when not in use (don’t leave it on just to keep Facebook or Myspace active).

Concept note-2: -Turning off the lights when you leave your room can help save energy. It can also help reduce carbon emission and other harmful greenhouse gases. Hence, turning off your lights is a simple way to help protect the environment and save the planet.

Concept note-3: -So, the correct answer is option B, reducing deforestation, cutting down use of fossil fuel.

Concept note-4: -Change a light. Replacing one regular light bulb with a compact fluorescent light bulb will save 150 pounds of carbon dioxide a year. Drive less. Recycle more. Check your tires.

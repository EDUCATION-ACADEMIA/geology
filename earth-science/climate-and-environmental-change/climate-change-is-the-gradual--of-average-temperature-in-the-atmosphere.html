Climate change is the gradual ____ of average temperature in the atmosphere.

(A) ** increase

(B) decrease

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global Warming is the gradual increase in the overall temperature of the Earth’s atmosphere. It begins with a phenomenon called the greenhouse effect. The greenhouse effect occurs when there is an interaction between Earth’s atmosphere and the radiation from the sun.

Concept note-2: -Global warming is basically a gradual increase in the average temperature of the earth’s lower atmosphere. It is primarily due to the trapping of heat by the greenhouse gases.

Concept note-3: -Since 1880, average global temperatures have increased by about 1 degrees Celsius (1.7° degrees Fahrenheit). Global temperature is projected to warm by about 1.5 degrees Celsius (2.7° degrees Fahrenheit) by 2050 and 2-4 degrees Celsius (3.6-7.2 degrees Fahrenheit) by 2100.

Concept note-4: -Climate change is defined as gradual changes in all the interconnected weather elements on our planet over approximately 30 years. The data shows the Earth is warming and it’s up to us to make the changes necessary for a healthier planet.

Concept note-5: -“Global warming is a gradual increase in the earth’s temperature generally due to the greenhouse effect caused by increased levels of carbon dioxide, CFCs, and other pollutants.

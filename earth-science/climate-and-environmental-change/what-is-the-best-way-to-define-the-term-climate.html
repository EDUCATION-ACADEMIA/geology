What is the best way to define the term “climate”?

(A) The conditions outside right now in a specific place

(B) A change in the average conditions (such as temperature and rainfall) in a region over a long period of time

(C) ** The weather conditions that are expected in a region at a particular time of year

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Climate, sometimes understood as the “average weather, ” is defined as the measurement of the mean and variability of relevant quantities of certain variables (such as temperature, precipitation or wind) over a period of time, ranging from months to thousands or millions of years.

Concept note-2: -Climate is the long-term pattern of weather in a particular area. Weather can change from hour-to-hour, day-to-day, month-to-month or even year-to-year. A region’s weather patterns, usually tracked for at least 30 years, are considered its climate.

Concept note-3: -* Weather is the condition of the atmosphere of a place at a particular time. * Climate is the average weather over a long period of time.

Concept note-4: -⚡ Quick summary: Weather refers to short-term atmospheric conditions-the temperature and precipitation on a certain day, for example. Climate refers to the average atmospheric conditions that prevail in a given region over a long period of time-whether a place is generally cold and wet or hot and dry, for example.

Concept note-5: -The day-to-day information of atmospheric changes of a particular area at a specific time is called weather. Climate is the statistical information of the average weather condition of a specific region for more than 30 years. 2. The weather of a place includes the short-term atmospheric condition.

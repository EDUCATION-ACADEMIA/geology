The negative effect of the increased rate of global warming can be addressed by

(A) using energy more efficiently

(B) reducing deforestation

(C) shifting to renewable energy sources

(D) ** all of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Higher temperatures are worsening many types of disasters, including storms, heat waves, floods, and droughts. A warmer climate creates an atmosphere that can collect, retain, and unleash more water, changing weather patterns in such a way that wet areas become wetter and dry areas drier.

Concept note-2: -Global warming can result in many serious alterations to the environment, eventually impacting human health. It can also cause a rise in sea level, leading to the loss of coastal land, a change in precipitation patterns, increased risks of droughts and floods, and threats to biodiversity.

Concept note-3: -Which one of the following is the effect of global warming? Explanation: Desertification is one of the effects of global warming.

Concept note-4: -Change a light. Replacing one regular light bulb with a compact fluorescent light bulb will save 150 pounds of carbon dioxide a year. Drive less. Recycle more. Check your tires. Use less hot water. Avoid products with a lot of packaging. Adjust your thermostat. Plant a tree. More items

Concept note-5: -Hotter temperatures. As greenhouse gas concentrations rise, so does the global surface temperature. More severe storms. Increased drought. A warming, rising ocean. Loss of species. Not enough food. More health risks. Poverty and displacement.

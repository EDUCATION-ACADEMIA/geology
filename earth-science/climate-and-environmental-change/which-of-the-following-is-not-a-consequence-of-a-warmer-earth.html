Which of the following is not a consequence of a warmer Earth?

(A) Rising Sea Levels

(B) A change in global weather patterns

(C) Increased heat related deaths

(D) ** None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Increased agricultural productivity worldwide.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The correct answer: The character which is not associated with global warming is a. Decreasing sea levels. One of the major effects of global warming is the increase in sea level due to the melting of the polar ice caps.

Concept note-3: -We already see effects scientists predicted, such as the loss of sea ice, melting glaciers and ice sheets, sea level rise, and more intense heat waves. Scientists predict global temperature increases from human-made greenhouse gases will continue. Severe weather damage will also increase and intensify.

Concept note-4: -A warming atmosphere affects more than just air temperatures: while heat waves and droughts are becoming more common and intense, rainstorms are also becoming more powerful, sometimes provoking dangerous floods.

Concept note-5: -Ocean water gets heated and expands. Low coastal areas will get flooded. Regional patterns or precipitation (rain and snow) will be affected. Glaciers and polar ice sheets will melt faster.

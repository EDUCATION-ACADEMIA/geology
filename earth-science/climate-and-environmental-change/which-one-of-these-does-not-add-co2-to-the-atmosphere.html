Which one of these does not add CO2 to the atmosphere?

(A) cooking with gas

(B) ** planting trees

(C) burning wood

(D) riding in a bus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, the correct option is ‘Photosynthesis by producers’.

Concept note-2: -So, the correct option is ‘Photosynthesis by producers’.

Concept note-3: -The Process of photosynthesis takes up Carbon dioxide and release oxygen. Hence photosynthesis does not add carbon dioxide to the carbon cycle.

Concept note-4: -During the process of photosynthesis trees pull carbon out of the atmosphere to make sugar, but they also release carbon dioxide back into the atmosphere through decomposition. Carbon and other gases within forests are captured and released on a cycle.

Concept note-5: -Carbon is released back into the atmosphere when organisms die, volcanoes erupt, fires blaze, fossil fuels are burned, and through a variety of other mechanisms.

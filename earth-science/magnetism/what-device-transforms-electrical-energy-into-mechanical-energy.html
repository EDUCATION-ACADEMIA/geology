What device transforms electrical energy into mechanical energy?

(A) Generator

(B) Transformer

(C) ** Electric Motor

(D) Compass

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Electric motor: An electric motor is an electrical device that converts electrical energy to mechanical energy.

Concept note-2: -The most common device for changing electrical energy into mechanical energy is the motor. Most motors use electromagnets to cause mechanical rotation, which can do work.

Concept note-3: -Motors consist of loops of wire in a magnetic field. When current is passed through the loops, the magnetic field exerts a torque on the loops, which rotates a shaft. Electrical energy is converted to mechanical work in the process.

Concept note-4: -An electric motor is an electrical machine that converts electrical energy into mechanical energy. Most electric motors operate through the interaction between the motor’s magnetic field and electric current in a wire winding to generate force in the form of torque applied on the motor’s shaft.

Which of these in an insulator?

(A) a copper wire

(B) a silver spoon

(C) ** a plastic coating on a wire

(D) an aluminum coating on a wire

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PVC is an insulator and prevents electricity to be conducted outside the wire so that electric shock can be prevented.

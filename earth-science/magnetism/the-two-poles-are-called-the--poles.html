The two poles are called the ____ poles

(A) east and west

(B) north and east

(C) south and west

(D) ** north and south

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A geographical pole or geographic pole is either of the two points on Earth where its axis of rotation intersects its surface. The North Pole lies in the Arctic Ocean while the South Pole is in Antarctica.

Concept note-2: -There are three North Poles: a geographic North Pole and a magnetic North Pole. There is also a geomagnetic North Pole, which is a very difficult scientific concept to understand. The geographic North Pole is located about 725 km north of Greenland in the middle of the Arctic Ocean. This is called true north.

Concept note-3: -Both the Arctic (North Pole) and the Antarctic (South Pole) are very cold because they get very little direct sunlight.

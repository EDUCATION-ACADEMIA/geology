MAGNETISM

[SOURCES]
If you placed the south pole of one bar magnet near the south pole of another bar magnet, the two magnets would ____ each other.

(A) attract

(B) cancel

(C) ** repel

(D) discharge

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Unlike poles of magnet attract each other while like poles repel. So, the south and the north pole of two magnets will attract each other. Q. The south and north poles of two magnets will attract each other.

Concept note-2: -When opposite poles of the magnets approach, they attract each other. Any north-south combination will pull them together. But when two like poles come together, they push each other apart.

Concept note-3: -When two of the same poles are near one another, they push away from one another. This means the two poles repel each other.

Concept note-4: -When you place like poles of two magnets near each other (north to north or south to south), they will repel each other.

Concept note-5: -If the north pole of a magnet is kept closer to the south pole of another magnet, then, they will repel each other.

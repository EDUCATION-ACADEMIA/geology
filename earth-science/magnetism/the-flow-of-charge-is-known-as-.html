EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The flow of charges is known as electric current.

Concept note-2: -The flow of charge is called electric current.

Concept note-4: -11 views • Jan 4, 2023. The rate of flow of charge in a circuit is called electric current. In other words, it is the amount of charge flowing per second. It is denoted by the letter I.

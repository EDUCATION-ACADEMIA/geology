The strength of a magnetic field produced by a current can be increased by ____ (Pick 2)

(A) ** Increasing the current

(B) Making the wire more straight

(C) ** Adding more coils

(D) Increasing the resistance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are two methods by which we can increase strength of magnetic field are: 1) By increasing the number of turns of wire in the coil. 2) By increasing the current flowing the coil.

Concept note-2: -The factors affecting the strength of a magnetic field at a point due to a straight current-carrying conductor are the magnitude of the electric current and the perpendicular distance between the point and the conductor.

Concept note-3: -As we increase the current (amount of charge in motion) the field increases proportionally. As we move further away from the wire, the field we see drops off proportionally with the distance. This is described by Ampereâ€™s law.

Concept note-4: -increasing the number of turns on the coil. increasing the current. placing an iron core inside the solenoid.

Concept note-5: -Increase the number of coil turns. Increase the current flowing through the coil. Wrap the coil tightly around the iron piece. Increase the current or voltage.

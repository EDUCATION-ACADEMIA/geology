Which best describes electromagnetism?

(A) the electrical component of a magnet

(B) the flow of electric charges between two magnets

(C) ** the interaction between electrical energy and magnetism

(D) the flow of a magnetic field between electrons

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The major difference between electricity and magnetism is their presence. Electricity can be present in a static charge, while magnetismâ€™s presence is only felt when there are moving charges as a result of electricity.

Concept note-2: -Electromagnetism is a branch of Physics, that deals with the electromagnetic force that occurs between electrically charged particles. The electromagnetic force is one of the four fundamental forces and exhibits electromagnetic fields such as magnetic fields, electric fields, and light.

Concept note-3: -Electricity and magnetism are closely related. Flowing electrons produce a magnetic field, and spinning magnets cause an electric current to flow. Electromagnetism is the interaction of these two important forces.

Concept note-4: -Electromagnetism involves two main relationships: electric currents produce magnetic fields, and. moving or changing the strength of magnetic fields can produce an electric current.

Concept note-5: -Some electromagnet uses in the home include an electric fan, electric doorbell, induction cooker, magnetic locks, etc. In an electric fan, the electromagnetic induction keeps the motor rotating on and on making the blade of the fan to rotate.

a insulator

a switch

a breaker

Detailed explanation-2: -When the switch is off, the circuit is incomplete and hence, current will not flow in the circuit. Q. If an electric switch is open then no current flows through the circuit.

Detailed explanation-3: -When the circuit switch is off, no electricity will flow and then the circuit is called an open circuit.

Which type of magnet is used to find geographic direction?

(A) Bar Magnet

(B) Horse Shoe Magnet

(C) Electromagnet

(D) ** Magnetic Compass

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Magnetic compass is used for navigation and its magnetic needle is made of permanent magnet which is always aligned according to the earth’s magnetic field.

Concept note-2: -magnetic compass, in navigation or surveying, an instrument for determining direction on the surface of Earth by means of a magnetic pointer that aligns itself with Earth’s magnetic field.

Concept note-3: -In a compass, only a magnetic needle is used that can rotate freely. When we keep a compass at a place, its magnetic needle will align itself in the north-south direction, because the earth also has its own magnetic field. Once the north-south direction is found then it is very easy to find the east-west direction too.

Concept note-4: -Q. A compass uses the directive property of a magnet. Q. (i) A compass is used to show the directions.

Concept note-5: -Directive property of magnet is used to prepare magnetic compass. Compass is an instrument which is used to find the directions. Compass consist of a tiny magnetic needle which is pivoted from the center and is free to rotate. The compass has a circular dial in which directions are marked.

MAGNETISM

[SOURCES]
Paper clips and staples are attracted to magnets because paper clips and staples are

(A) permanent magnets.

(B) temporary magnets.

(C) ** magnetic materials.

(D) magnetic domains.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Magnets are attracted to items made of iron, cobalt, nickel, and steel. Paperclips are traditionally made of galvanized steel wire, which means paperclips are magnetic.

Concept note-2: -Magnets have the ability to attract certain metals like iron and steel. Metal paper clips are made from steel and should be attracted by a magnet.

Concept note-3: -Making Magnets: The Basics It can attract small objects, like paper clips and staples.

Concept note-4: -The effect of magnetism is the strongest at the ends of a magnet, i.e. at the poles of the magnet. Hence the clips will mostly stick to the poles of the magnet.

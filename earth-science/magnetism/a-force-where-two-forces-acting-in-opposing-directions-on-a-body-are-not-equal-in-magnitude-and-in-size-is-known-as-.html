A force where two forces acting in opposing directions on a body, are not equal in magnitude and in size, is known as ____

(A) Gravity

(B) Balanced Force

(C) ** Unbalanced Force

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Unbalanced force is a force that occurs when two forces acting in opposite directions on a body are not equal in magnitude and size.

Concept note-2: -Equal forces acting in opposite directions are called balanced forces. Balanced forces acting on an object will not change the objectâ€™s motion. When you add equal forces in opposite direction, the net force is zero.

Concept note-3: -forces: forces applied to an object in opposite directions that are not equal in size.

Concept note-4: -What are Unbalanced Forces? When the resultant force acting on a body is not equal to zero, the forces acting on the body are known as unbalanced forces. The body acted upon by unbalanced forces changes its state of motion.

Concept note-5: -When two unequal forces act in opposite directions on an object, the net force is the difference of the two forces. The final force and its direction are called a resultant.

Magnetic Field lines ____

(A) ** Go out of the N side and into the S side

(B) Go out of the S side and into the N side

(C) Go out of the N side AND out of the S side

(D) Go into the N side AND go into the S side

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Magnetic field lines are the imaginary lines around a magnet which gives us the pattern of magnetic field of the magnet. These lines donâ€™t start or end. They form a closed loop. They flow from North pole to South pole outside the magnet, while they flow from South pole to North pole inside the magnet.

Concept note-2: -Magnetic field lines appear to move from the South pole towards the North pole inside a magnet.

Concept note-3: -Inside the magnet, the direction of field lines is from its south pole to its north pole. Thus the magnetic field lines are closed curves.

Concept note-4: -The north pole and south pole are the two poles of a magnet.

Concept note-5: -Thus, the magnetic field lines point away from the north pole of a magnet and toward its south pole.

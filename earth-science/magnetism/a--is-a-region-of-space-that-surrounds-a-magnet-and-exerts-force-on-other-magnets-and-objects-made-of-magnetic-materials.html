A ____ is a region of space that surrounds a magnet and exerts force on other magnets and objects made of magnetic materials

(A) magnetism

(B) ** magnetic field

(C) magnetic pole

(D) magnetic domain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The magnetic field is the area around a magnet that has magnetic force. All magnets have north and south poles . Opposite poles are attracted to each other, while the same poles repel each other.

Concept note-2: -A magnetic field is a region of space that surrounds a magnet and exerts a force on other magnets and objects made of magnetic materials. The magnetic field and the magnetic force are related. A stronger magnetic field means that a magnetic object placed in the field will experience a stronger magnetic force.

Concept note-3: -The space around a magnet where its influence can be detected, is called magnetic field. The magnetic field is filled with magnetic line of forces.

Concept note-4: -The areas of magnets that have magnetic strength are called “poles.” When we have more than one magnet poles repel, pull or attract each other.

Concept note-5: -A space or region around a magnet in which a force is experienced by a magnetic pole is called magnetic field.

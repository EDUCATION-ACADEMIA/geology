What are the original substances that are mixed together to produce a chemical change?

(A) products

(B) chemicals

(C) ** reactants

(D) mixtures

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The substances that go into a chemical reaction are called the reactants, and the substances produced at the end of the reaction are known as the products.

Concept note-2: -The substance (or substances) initially involved in a chemical reaction are called reactants or reagents.

Concept note-3: -A mixture is created when two or more different substances are physically combined and can be separated back into its original substances. A chemical reaction occurs when two or more substances are combined to form a new substance and cannot be separated back into its original substances.

Concept note-4: -A combination reaction is a reaction in which two reactants combine to form one product. Oxygen and the halogens are very reactive elements and are likely to undergo combination reactions with other elements.

Concept note-5: -Synthesis. When making a new substance from other substances, chemists say either that they carry out a synthesis or that they synthesize the new material. Reactants are converted to products, and the process is symbolized by a chemical equation.

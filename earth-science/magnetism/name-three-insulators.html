Name three insulators.

(A) ** plastic, wood, rubber

(B) glass, ice, paper

(C) wood, paper, water

(D) oil, water, wax

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Insulators: Wood, plastic, rubber, glass, distilled water.

Concept note-2: -Some common insulator materials are glass, plastic, rubber, air, and wood. Insulators are used to protect us from the dangerous effects of electricity flowing through conductors.

Concept note-3: -Wood is a natural insulator. Wood has a lower thermal conductivity compared to concrete, steel-frame and masonry construction and is ideally suited to energy-efficient design. As a natural insulator, it provides some of the world’s coldest climates with warm and comfortable dwellings.

Concept note-4: -Main role of an insulator is to keep electricity within the material and rubber is the best material because of that it’s used in the electric wire. Rubber is the best insulator that could be molded to shape and extruded.

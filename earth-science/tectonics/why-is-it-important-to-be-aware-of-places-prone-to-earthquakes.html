Why is it important to be aware of places prone to earthquakes?

(A) to identify what crop must be stored

(B) to identify when to evacuate

(C) to locate where to stay best

(D) ** to perform necessary precautions

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is important to identify areas that are prone to earthquakes in order to prepare for the possibility that they may occur. This preparation takes the form of disaster planning and community planning, but most importantly involves infrastructure.

Concept note-2: -With a greater understanding of the causes and effects of earthquakes and how they impact buildings, infrastructure, and society itself, we may be able to reduce damage and loss of life from this destructive phenomenon.

Concept note-3: -Earthquakes strike suddenly, violently and without warning, but identifying potential hazards and planning can reduce the threat of damage, injuries and loss of life.

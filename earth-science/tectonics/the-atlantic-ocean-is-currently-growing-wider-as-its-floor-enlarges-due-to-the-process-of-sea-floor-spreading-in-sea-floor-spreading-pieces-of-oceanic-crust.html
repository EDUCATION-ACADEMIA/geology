The Atlantic Ocean is currently growing wider as its floor enlarges due to the process of sea-floor spreading. In sea-floor spreading, pieces of oceanic crust

(A) move sideways against each other along a transform plate boundary.

(B) move down in a subduction zone.

(C) move together along a convergent plate boundary.

Concept note-1: -The Atlantic Ocean is currently growing wider as its floor enlarges due to the process of sea-floor spreading. In sea-floor spreading, pieces of oceanic crust. move sideways against each other along a transform plate boundary.

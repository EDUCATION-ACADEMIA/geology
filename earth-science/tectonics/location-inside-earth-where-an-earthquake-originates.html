Location inside Earth where an earthquake originates:

(A) Fissure

(B) ** Focus

(C) Epicenter

(D) Fault

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The point where an earthquake originates is called the seismic focus. It is also known as hypocenter.

Concept note-2: -The earthquakes originate in tectonic plate boundary. The focus is point inside the earth where the earthquake started, sometimes called the hypocenter, and the point on the surface of the earth directly above the focus is called the epicenter.

Concept note-3: -Earthquakes occur in the crust or upper mantle, which ranges from the earthâ€™s surface to about 800 kilometers deep (about 500 miles).

Concept note-4: -The crust is made of solid rocks and minerals. Beneath the crust is the mantle, which is also mostly solid rocks and minerals, but punctuated by malleable areas of semi-solid magma. At the center of the Earth is a hot, dense metal core.

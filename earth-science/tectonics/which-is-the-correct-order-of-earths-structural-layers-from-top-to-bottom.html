Which is the correct order of Earth’s structural layers, from top to bottom?

(A) ** lithosphere, asthenosphere, mesosphere, outer core, inner core

(B) outer core, inner core, mesosphere, asthenosphere, lithosphere

(C) asthenosphere, mesosphere, lithosphere, outer core, inner core

(D) outer core, inner core, lithosphere, mesosphere, asthenosphere

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mechanically, the earth’s layers can be divided into lithosphere, asthenosphere, mesospheric mantle (part of the Earth’s mantle below the lithosphere and the asthenosphere), outer core, and inner core. Chemically, Earth can be divided into the crust, upper mantle, lower mantle, outer core, and inner core.

Concept note-2: -They are, in order from the exterior to the interior – the crust, the mantle, the outer core, and the inner core.

Concept note-3: -Starting at the center, Earth is composed of four distinct layers. They are, from deepest to shallowest, the inner core, the outer core, the mantle and the crust.

Concept note-4: -Crust, mantle, core, lithosphere, asthenosphere, mesosphere, outer core, inner core.

Concept note-5: -These five layers are the: Lithosphere, Asthenosphere, Mesosphere, Outer Core, and Inner Core.

Newton’s laws of motion apply to everyday life in a variety of ways. Which one of the followingstatements does not demonstrate Newton’s laws of motion as they relate to plate tectonics? F Plates resist change as in Newton’s law of inertia. G The greater the force, the greater rate of acceleration as in Newton’s law of acceleration. H Volcanoes form from cooled magma as in Newton’s law of acceleration. J Plates exert a force on each other in opposite directions as in Newton’s law of action-reaction.

(A) F

(B) G

(C) ** H

(D) J

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Newton’s laws of motion apply to everyday life in a variety of ways. Which one of the followingstatements does not demonstrate Newton’s laws of motion as they relate to plate tectonics? F Plates resist change as in Newton’s law of inertia.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is option 3 i.e Ocean current.

Concept note-3: -The North American and Eurasian Plates are moving away from each other along the line of the Mid Atlantic Ridge. The Ridge extends into the South Atlantic Ocean between the South American and African Plates.

Concept note-4: -Which of the following statements is NOT supported by the plate tectonic theory? Continents move through the seafloor crust.

Concept note-5: -Plate tectonics is the scientific theory explaining the movement of the earth’s crust. It is widely accepted by scientists today.

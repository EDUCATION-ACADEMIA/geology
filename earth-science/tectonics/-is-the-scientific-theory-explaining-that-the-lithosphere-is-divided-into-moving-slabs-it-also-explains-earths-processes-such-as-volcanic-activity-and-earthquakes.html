____ is the scientific theory explaining that the lithosphere is divided into moving slabs. It also explains Earth’s processes, such as volcanic activity and earthquakes.

(A) The Hypothesis of Continental drift

(B) ** The Theory of Plate Tectonics

(C) The Theory of Moving Slabs

(D) The Theory of Drifting Plates

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tectonic Plates The system of ideas behind plate tectonics theory suggests that Earth’s outer shell (lithosphere) is divided into several plates that glide over the Earth’s rocky inner layer above the soft core (mantle).

Concept note-2: -The theory of plate tectonics revolutionized the earth sciences by explaining how the movement of geologic plates causes mountain building, volcanoes, and earthquakes.

Concept note-3: -Plate tectonics (from the Late Latin: tectonicus, from the Ancient Greek: , lit. ‘pertaining to building’) is the generally accepted scientific theory that considers the Earth’s lithosphere to comprise a number of large tectonic plates which have been slowly moving since about 3.4 billion years ago.

Concept note-4: -The theory of plate tectonics is what brings together continental drift and seafloor spreading. Plates are made of lithosphere topped with oceanic and/or continental crust. The plates are moved around on Earth’s surface by seafloor spreading. Convection in the mantle drives seafloor spreading.

What does the author compare the earth’s surface to?

(A) dinner plates

(B) ** a massive puzzle

(C) the ocean

(D) an earthquake

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. Explanation: The world fits together so because of that its simple to compared to a massive puzzle, that once fit together.

Concept note-2: -Answer: The poet compared the earth with the patient because earth is being polluted by us (human beings).

Concept note-3: -what are tectonic plates? Tectonic plates are gigantic pieces of the Earth’s crust and uppermost mantle. They are made up of oceanic crust and continental crust.

Concept note-4: -two ways in which changes on the earth’s surface are connected to changes below the earth’s surface. 1: Convection currents cause chain reactions that pull plates apart on land. 2: Severe earthquakes are formed as two plates slide past each other.

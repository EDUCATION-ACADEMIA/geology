Ann and her father find a 30 million year old tropical plant fossil in Louisiana. Which question does the fossil help them answer?

(A) Were there mountains in Louisiana 30 years ago?

(B) ** What was the climate like in Louisiana 30 years ago?

(C) Were there earthquakes in Louisiana 30 years ago?

(D) What type of animal lived in Louisiana 30 years ago?

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The fossil plants are indicative that the area had a warm climate without extended periods of winter temperatures below freezing, along with adequate moisture in the air for growth.

Concept note-2: -This method is called radiometric dating, and it involves the decay, or breakdown, of radioactive elements. Using radiometric dating techniques, it became possible to determine the actual age of a sample.

Concept note-3: -Which best explains how index fossils found in sedimentary rock layers can be used to determine the approximate age of a rock layer? Each index fossil was formed during every geologic time period.

Concept note-4: -Index fossils are useful because they tell the relative ages of the rock layers in which they occur. Geologists use particular types of organisms, such as trilobites, as index fossils.

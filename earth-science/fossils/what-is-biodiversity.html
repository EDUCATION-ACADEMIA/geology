What is biodiversity?

(A) a rapid increase in the number of living things on Earth

(B) the history of life on Earth, as documented by fossils

(C) ** the variety of species on Earth

(D) all of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Biodiversity is all the different kinds of life youâ€™ll find in one area-the variety of animals, plants, fungi, and even microorganisms like bacteria that make up our natural world. Each of these species and organisms work together in ecosystems, like an intricate web, to maintain balance and support life.

Concept note-2: -Biodiversity is the natural world around us, and the variety of all of the different kinds of organisms-the plants, animals, insects and microorganisms that live on our planet.

Concept note-3: -Biodiversity refers to the variety of living species on Earth, including plants, animals, bacteria, and fungi.

Concept note-4: -Biodiversity is the variation among living organisms from different sources including terrestrial, marine and desert ecosystems, and the ecological complexes of which they are a part.

Concept note-5: -Usually three levels of biodiversity are discussed-genetic, species, and ecosystem diversity.

Which of the following is a requirement of index fossils?

(A) Lived over a vast time period but found only in one location

(B) ** lived only for a short time but was found all over the world

(C) Not a prevalent species at any time

(D) Still alive today

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A useful index fossil must be distinctive or easily recognizable, abundant, and have a wide geographic distribution and a short range through time. Index fossils are the basis for defining boundaries in the geologic time scale and for the correlation of strata.

Concept note-2: -Index fossils are fossils that are widespread but only existed for a short period of time.

Concept note-3: -An index fossil must be distinctive, widespread, and short-lived. It indicates a specific period of time.

Concept note-4: -Index fossils are of organisms that lived over a wide area. They lived for a fairly short period of time.

(A) similarities in DNA

(B) similarities in early development

(C) similarities in body structure

(D) ** similarities in number of mates

Concept note-1: -The correct answer to the question is option D which is Similarities in a number of mates.

Concept note-4: -Human Evolution Scientists who study early humans depend on fossil evidence to help them sort out how our ancestors evolved over time.

Properties mean

(A) talents that someone has

(B) ** qualities of something

(C) student grades

(D) jobs that workers have

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -prop·er·ty ˈprä-pər-tē plural properties. Synonyms of property. : a quality or trait belonging and especially peculiar to an individual or thing. : an effect that an object has on another object or on the senses.

Concept note-2: -1. something of value, either tangible, such as land, or intangible, such as patents, copyrights, etc. 2. law. the right to possess, use, and dispose of anything.

Concept note-3: -Definitions of property. something owned; any tangible or intangible possession that is owned by someone. “that hat is my property” “he is a man of property” synonyms: belongings, holding.

Concept note-4: -Some common synonyms of property are attribute, character, and quality.

Concept note-5: -A quality or feature that is characteristic of someone or something is one which is often seen in them and seems typical of them.

Where can we locate Mt. St. Tomas?

(A) Kalinga

(B) Apayao

(C) Benguet

(D) ** Mt.Province

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Parangimalai (known in English as St. Thomas Mount) is a small hillock in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, near the neighbourhood of Guindy and very close to Chennai International Airport.

Concept note-2: -AC St. Thomas Mount | Chennai District | India.

Concept note-3: -1900. The mount is 11 km south of Madras and is traditionally held to be the site of the martyrdom of the apostle St Thomas. It is thought that he founded Christian communities on the west coast of India and came to Mylapore, where he was killed in 68 AD.

Concept note-4: -One of the earliest memorials in Chennai is the peaceful little Portuguese chapel on top of the hill (a flight of 132 steps leads to the summit) and built at the site where St. Thomas, the Apostle of India, was martyred in 72 A.D. and thereafter the hill has been famously known as the St. Thomas Mount.

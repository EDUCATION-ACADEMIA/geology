What level of a house do you go to if there is a volcano?

(A) upstairs

(B) main level

(C) ** basement

(D) doesn’t madder

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Get Underneath Volcano Manor From the entrance hall of the mansion, turn right down the corridor and take the first door on your right, opened by using the Drawing-Room Key. In the back-right corner of the room, behind the body, there’s an illusory wall. Attack it to reveal an entrance.

Concept note-2: -In the room next door to Zoryas, you’ll find a hidden passage behind an illusory wall (or hidden wall or secret wall) in the back right corner, where the crooked painting hangs. Roll into the wall to dispel the illusion, and then make your way into Volcano Manor’s secret corridors.

Concept note-3: -Take temporary shelter from volcanic ash in the location where you are, if you have enough supplies. Cover ventilation openings and seal doors and windows. If outside, protect yourself from falling ash that can irritate skin and injure breathing passages, eyes and open wounds.

Concept note-4: -What is a volcano? A volcano is an opening in the Earth’s crust where magma – a mixture of red-hot liquid rock, mineral crystals, rock fragments and dissolved gases from inside the planet erupts onto the surface.

Recycling materials help save the following,

(A) Energy

(B) Landfill garbage

(C) Neither

(D) ** Both

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A: It conserves energy, reduces air and water pollution, reduces greenhouse gases, and conserves natural resources. Stanford recycled, composted, and otherwise source reduced 62% of its waste and reduced landfill by 35%.

Concept note-2: -Recycling reduces waste sent to landfills and incinerators. Recycling prevents pollution. Recycling conserves natural resources. Recycling conserves energy. Recycling creates jobs, producing economic benefits. 14-Jan-2020

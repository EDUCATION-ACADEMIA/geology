The crystal system that has three axes with equal length but in which none of the axes meet at a 90o angle is ____

(A) Cubic

(B) ** Trigonal or Rhombohedral

(C) Monoclinic

(D) Hexagonal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The rhombohedral lattice is also known as trigonal, and has no angles equal to 90°, but all sides are of equal length (a = b = c), thus requiring only by one lattice parameter, and all three angles are equal ( = = ).

Concept note-2: -The Isometric System The first and simplest crystal system is the isometric or cubic system. It has three axes, all of which are the same length. The three axes in the isometric system all intersect at 90º to each other.

Concept note-3: -Orthorhombic: Three mutually perpendicular axes of different perpendicular axes of different lengths.

Concept note-4: -In orthorhombic crystal system all angles are 90 degrees but the three edge lengths are not equal.

Concept note-5: -monoclinic system, one of the structural categories to which crystalline solids can be assigned. Crystals in this system are referred to three axes of unequal lengths-say, a, b, and c-of which a is perpendicular to b and c, but b and c are not perpendicular to each other.

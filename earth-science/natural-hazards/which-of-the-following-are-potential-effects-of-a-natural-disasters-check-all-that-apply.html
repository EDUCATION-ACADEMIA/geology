Which of the following are potential effects of a natural disasters (check all that apply)?

(A) ** spread of disease due to reduced standards of sanitation

(B) ** disruption of communication networks

(C) ** disruption of government and its services

(D) ** loss of jobs and businesses

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -These disasters result in disruption through damage to property, physical injury and death, psychological distress, displacement of individuals and families, and prolonged disruption to a broad range of services upon which communities rely.

Concept note-2: -A natural disaster may cause loss of life, injury or other health impacts, property damage, loss of livelihoods and services, social and economic disruption, or environmental damage.

Concept note-3: -In general three outcomes are possible: recovery, stagnation, or decline.

Concept note-4: -The immediate effects of a natural disaster materialize through four main channels: loss of life; destruction of physical capital (e.g. housing, commercial buildings, vehicles, andc infrastructure such as roads); population displacement; and disruption of economic activity.

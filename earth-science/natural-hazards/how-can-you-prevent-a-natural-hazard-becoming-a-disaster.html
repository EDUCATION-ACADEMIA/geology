How can you prevent a natural hazard becoming a disaster?

(A) Avoid unnecessary ricks

(B) Plan ahead and keep an emergency kit on hand

(C) Follow the advice of local authorities

(D) ** All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Raising awareness about potential hazards and how to address them. Educating the public about how to properly prepare for different types of disaster. Installing and strengthening prediction and warning systems. 03-Sept-2021

Concept note-2: -By issuing accurate forecasts and warnings in a form that is readily understood and by educating people on how to prepare against such hazards, before they become disasters, lives and property can be protected.

Concept note-3: -Can We Prevent Disasters? We can’t stop natural phenomena from happening. But we can make them less damaging if we understand better why they happen, and what we can do to prevent or mitigate them.

Concept note-4: -Move inland to higher ground immediately and stay there. Check for a noticeable recession in water away from the shoreline as this is nature’s tsunami warning and should be heeded. It would help if you moved away immediately. Please stay away from flooded and damaged areas until officials say it is safe to return.

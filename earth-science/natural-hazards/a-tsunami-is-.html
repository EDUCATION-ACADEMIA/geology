A tsunami is ____

(A) a spinning windstorm that forms over the ocean.

(B) ** a series of ocean waves.

(C) a vibration deep within the Earth or at its surface.

(D) when magma rises through the Earthâ€™s surface.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A tsunami is a series of extremely long waves caused by a large and sudden displacement of the ocean, usually the result of an earthquake below or near the ocean floor. This force creates waves that radiate outward in all directions away from their source, sometimes crossing entire ocean basins.

Concept note-2: -The correct option is A a tsunami. A tsunami is series of enormous and fast moving waves in the ocean caused by powerful earthquakes or volcanic eruptions.

Concept note-3: -Tsunami may reach a maximum vertical height onshore above sea level, called a run-up height, of 30 meters. A notable exception is the landslide generated tsunami in Lituya Bay, Alaska in 1958 which produced a 60 meter high wave.

Concept note-4: -Tsunamis are giant waves caused by earthquakes or volcanic eruptions under the sea. Out in the depths of the ocean, tsunami waves do not dramatically increase in height. But as the waves travel inland, they build up to higher and higher heights as the depth of the ocean decreases.

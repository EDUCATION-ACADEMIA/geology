Choose the best 2 phrases. Strange animal behavior, such as toads fleeing an area, could be an indicator that ____ are/is about to occur.

(A) a volcanic eruption

(B) ** seismic waves

(C) a hurricane

(D) ** an earthquake

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to researchers, animals like dog, cat and cow are believed to detect disasters.

Concept note-2: -Earthquakes are usually caused when underground rock suddenly breaks and there is rapid motion along a fault. This sudden release of energy causes the seismic waves that make the ground shake.

Concept note-3: -Going forward, there is also a possibility that unforeseen events (i.e. major earthquakes in the Tokyo metropolitan area or along the Nankai Trough, outbreaks of infectious diseases, or volcanic eruptions) may occur on a scale that exceeds damage estimates carefully determined by the BCP.

Concept note-4: -Predictions and warnings can also reduce damage and economic losses. When notice of an impending disaster can be issued well in advance, as it can for some riverine floods, wildfires, and hurricanes, property and natural resources can be protected.

Natural hazards can cause a variety of problems. Which type of destruction can be caused only by volcanic activity?Choose the correct answer.

(A) damage from flooding and wind

(B) erosion of land along coasts and rivers

(C) sudden crumbling of buildings and chimneys

(D) ** areas being covered by ash and concrete-like mudslides

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Natural disasters disrupt people’s lives through displacements, destruction of livelihoods and property, deaths, and injuries.

Concept note-2: -Natural hazards are physical phenomena caused by atmospheric, water or tectonic processes that threaten people, property or the environment. They can occur within a short or long period of time.

Concept note-3: -They include cyclones, lightning, drought, avalanches, hail storms, tornadoes, floods, heatwaves. They result from the state of Earth’s atmosphere and its interaction with lands and oceans and the weather and climate it produces.

Concept note-4: -Study Notes. Natural hazard. Atmospheric hazard. Geophysical hazard. Hydrological hazards. 22-Mar-2021

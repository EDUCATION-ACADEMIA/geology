What are some natural disasters?

(A) A war

(B) hurricanes, earthquakes, tornadoes, tsunamis, and rain

(C) ** hurricanes, earthquakes, tornadoes, tsunamis, and volcanoes

(D) A very bad day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Examples of natural hazards include: avalanche, coastal flooding, cold wave, drought, earthquake, hail, heat wave, hurricane (tropical cyclone), ice storm, landslide, lightning, riverine flooding, strong wind, tornado, typhoon, tsunami, volcanic activity, wildfire, winter weather.

Concept note-2: -Geological Disasters, Hydrological Disasters, Meteorological Disasters, and Biological Disasters are the four broad types of natural hazards.

Concept note-3: -Hurricanes and tropical storms. Landslides & debris flow. Thunderstorms and lighting. Tornadoes. Tsunamis. Wildfire. Winter and ice storms. Sinkholes.

Concept note-4: -Avalanche. Coastal Flooding. Cold Wave. Drought. Earthquake. Hail.

Concept note-5: -Earthquakes. Volcanic eruptions. Landslides. Tsunamis. Avalanches. Floods. Heat waves. Cold waves. Wildfires. Droughts. Cyclones. Epidemics and pandemics. Technological and biological hazards. Hailstorms.

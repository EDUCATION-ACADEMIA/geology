A ____ can be used to keep your house and/electricity safe during a thunderstorm.

(A) ** lightning rod

(B) roof

(C) tall tree

(D) basement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lightning rods (and the accompanying protection system) are designed to protect a house or building from a direct lightning strike and, in particular, a lightning-initiated fire.

Concept note-2: -The rods stick atop the highest point of the building, aiming to attract the lightning bolt. Once the bolt strikes, the rod safely channels the millions of volts of energy through copper or aluminum cables around the home and into the ground.

A person trained to in space travel is an ____

(A) astrologist

(B) ** astronaut

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An astronaut is someone who travels in space. While the term was once reserved for military-trained professionals, recent accessibility of space travel has seen the term astronaut now used to refer to anyone traveling in a spacecraft, including civilians.

Concept note-2: -astronaut, designation, derived from the Greek words for “star” and “sailor, ” commonly applied to an individual who has flown in outer space. More specifically, in the West, astronaut refers to those from the United States, Canada, Europe, and Japan who travel into space.

Concept note-3: -A professional space traveler is called an astronaut.

Concept note-4: -There are three types of astronauts in the U.S. space program: the commander/pilot, mission specialist, and payload specialist.

Concept note-1: -Waves are most commonly caused by wind. Wind-driven waves, or surface waves, are created by the friction between wind and surface water. As wind blows across the surface of the ocean or a lake, the continual disturbance creates a wave crest.

Concept note-2: -The sun is the main source of energy for the oceans, both directly, through light and heat energy, and indirectly, by heating the air to produce winds. The oceans also receive energy from the pull exerted on the Earth by the moon, planets, and the sun.

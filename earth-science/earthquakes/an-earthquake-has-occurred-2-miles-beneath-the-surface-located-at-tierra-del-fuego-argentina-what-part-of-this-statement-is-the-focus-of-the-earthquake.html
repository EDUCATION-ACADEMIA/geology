“An earthquake has occurred 2 miles beneath the surface located at Tierra Del Fuego, Argentina.” What part of this statement is the focus of the earthquake?

(A) ** “ ____ 2 miles beneath the surface ____ “

(B) “An earthquake has occurred ____ “ Click to v Click to view"An earthquake has occurred ____ “ iew"An earthquake has occurred ____ “

(C) “ ____ located at Tierra Del Fuego, Argentina.”

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The focus is point inside the earth where the earthquake started, sometimes called the hypocenter, and the point on the surface of the earth directly above the focus is called the epicenter.

Concept note-2: -The location below the earth’s surface where the earthquake starts is called the hypocenter, and the location directly above it on the surface of the earth is called the epicenter. Sometimes an earthquake has foreshocks.

Concept note-3: -The focus is the place inside Earth’s crust where an earthquake originates. The point on the Earth’s surface directly above the focus is the epicenter. When energy is released at the focus, seismic waves travel outward from that point in all directions.

Concept note-4: -The Earth’s crust (the outer layer of the planet) is made up of several pieces called tectonic plates and most earthquakes occur along their edges. The plates under the oceans are called oceanic plates. Plates that are not under the ocean are continental plates.

Solve for wave speed given the following variables and using the formula Wave speed=frequency x wavelength. Frequency=16.35Hz, Wavelength is 21m

(A) 345.78 m/s

(B) ** 343.35 m/s

(C) 1.28 m/s

(D) 0.79 m/s

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Speed = Wavelength x Wave Frequency. In this equation, wavelength is measured in meters and frequency is measured in hertz (Hz), or number of waves per second. Therefore, wave speed is given in meters per second, which is the SI unit for speed.

Concept note-2: -Velocity = Wavelength x Frequency EXAMPLE: A wave as a Wavelength of 5 meters and a Frequency of 10 Hz. What is its velocity?

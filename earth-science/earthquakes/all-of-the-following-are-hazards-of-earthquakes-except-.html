EARTHQUAKES

[SOURCES]
All of the following are hazards of earthquakes except ____

(A) tsunamis

(B) landslides

(C) liquefaction

(D) ** volcanoes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The primary after effects of earthquakes are ground shaking, ground rupture, landslides, tsunamis, liquefaction, destruction of livelihood, death and injuries. Rainfall is not an after-effect of earthquake.

Concept note-2: -Earthquakes can cause damage by shaking, tsunami’s, and landslides. Volcanoes can cause damage by ash flows, release of gases, mudflows, lava flows, and landslides.

Concept note-3: -Ground Rupture. Deformation on the ground that marks, the intersection of the fault with the earth’s surface. Ground Shaking. Disruptive up, down and sideways vibration of the ground during an earthquake. Liquefaction. Earthquake-induced Landslide. Tsunami.

Concept note-4: -ground shaking. landslides. liquefaction. surface rupture.

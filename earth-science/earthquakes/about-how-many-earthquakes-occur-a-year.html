About how many earthquakes occur a year?

(A) 900, 000

(B) 5, 000

(C) 88, 000

(D) ** 500, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is estimated that around 500, 000 earthquakes occur each year, detectable with current instrumentation. About 100, 000 of these can be felt.

Concept note-2: -The National Earthquake Information Center now locates about 20, 000 earthquakes around the globe each year, or approximately 55 per day.

Concept note-3: -It is estimated that there are 500, 000 detectable earthquakes in the world each year. 100, 000 of those can be felt, and 100 of them cause damage.

Concept note-4: -The world’s greatest earthquake belt, the circum-Pacific seismic belt, is found along the rim of the Pacific Ocean, where about 81 percent of our planet’s largest earthquakes occur.

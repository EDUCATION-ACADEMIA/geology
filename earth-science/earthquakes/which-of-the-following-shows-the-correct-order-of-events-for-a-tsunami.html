Which of the following shows the correct order of events for a tsunami?

(A) Water recedes back into the ocean carrying all the debris, tsunami waves hit, tide retreats as a warning

(B) Tide retreats as a warning, water recedes back into the ocean carrying all the debris, tsunami waves hit

(C) ** Tide retreats as a warning, tsunami waves hit, water recedes back into the ocean carrying all the debris

(D) I have NO idea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are four levels of tsunami alerts: warning, advisory, watch, and information statement.

Concept note-2: -A tsunami has four general stages: initiation, split, amplification, and run-up. During initiation, a large set of ocean waves are caused by any large and sudden disturbance of the sea surface, most commonly earthquakes but sometimes also underwater landslides.

Concept note-3: -The life of a tsunami is usually divided into three phases: the generation (tsunami source), the propagation and the inundation. Each phase is complex and often described separately.

Concept note-4: -A tsunami begins far offshore, with an earthquake, volcanic eruption or landslide. The sudden movement on the seabed displaces the water above it. Although the vertical movement may initially be less than a metre, it covers a large area and the total volume of water displaced is huge.
